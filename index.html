BankLog

BankLog is an Italian project, founded by Gianluca Parri in the 2017's summer. It's a platform that can help people trace their money, and it is fully compatible with Third Party apps (so if You're a Web App Developer, or a Software Engineer, You'll surely enjoy the BankLog API). Nowdays, this project is mantained and daily updated by a team of 4 people (Gianluca Parri, me, Emanuele Lillo and Francesco De Benedittis).
