Team Info

Monolix is a start-up Software House, founded by Gianluca Parri in 2018. As of now, the team consists of five people: Gianluca, Emanuele, Giulian, Francesco and me.
We are currently working on some projects, public or not, such as BankLog.

Team Projects

BankLog

BankLog is an Italian project, founded by Gianluca Parri in the 2017's summer. It's a platform that can help people trace their money, and it is fully compatible with third party apps (so if you're a web app developer, or a software engineer, you'll surely enjoy the BankLog API). Nowadays, this project is mantained and daily updated by the Monolix team.
