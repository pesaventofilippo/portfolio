Project Info

A Telegram Bot to post, like and dislike photos in a group. You can put it in your photography-related group, and every user can post a photo trough this bot to immediately have a feedback from other users!
I've done it as a complete side-project and for fun, so it's not very optimized and functional. I might improve and update it in the future, so stay tuned!
